About Us

We, at the Enterpreneurship Cell of DHSK, strive to generate enthusiasm and awareness about start-ups among the peeps.

Aim

To encourage students to instill an enterpreneurial mindset and provide possible guidance along the way.

Vision

To create a self sustaining entrepreneurial community in our college which can work towards making it a better place to be in.

Verticals of Our E-Cell

Our E-Cell comprises of 3 verticals as of now for all the functioning and activities pertaining to promotion of enterpreneurial culture in DHSK College.

Branding Team

* Primarily focused on enhancing the brand value of E-Cell DHSK by managing its official social media accounts.

* Designing and creating content for social media accounts.

* Promotion of various intra and inter college events and talks that are organized within the cell.

* Publicity of various achievements of alums in the field of enterpreneurship.

Technical Team

* Provide an interactive platform for students to get connected with people of our own college through our official website.

* Development and maintainence of portals for Campus Director Programs, Internships, Start-Up Services, etc.

* Responsible for building and handling of the website.

Event Team

* Handling and organizing all the events, webinars and workshops conducted by the E-Cell during the course of the session.

* Reaching out to the speakers for sessions, talks, webinars, and workshops.

* Work for the marketing and publicity for every competition organized by E-Cell.

* Responsible for managing all the logistics, operations and hospitality of all the events.
